Stage 1

    Stage 1 remapping is a safe and efficient way to unlock your vehicle’s potential without requiring any hardware modifications.

    Perfect for:

  • Drivers looking for a noticeable performance boost.
  • Cars with factory settings and no additional upgrades.

    • Benefits:

  • Increased power and smoother acceleration.
  • Improved fuel efficiency under normal driving conditions.
  • Maintains the reliability and safety of your vehicle.

Stage 2

    Stage 2 remapping takes performance to the next level, designed for vehicles with specific hardware upgrades such as an upgraded exhaust system, intercooler, or air intake.

    Perfect for:

  • Enthusiasts who have upgraded their car’s hardware for enhanced performance.
  • Those looking for more aggressive tuning and optimized driving dynamics.

    • Benefits:

  • Significant increases in horsepower and torque.
  • Enhanced throttle response and overall driveability.
  • Tailored to your hardware setup for maximum results.

EGR Solutions

    EGR systems are designed to reduce harmful emissions, but over time, they can become clogged and cause performance issues such as reduced power, poor fuel economy, and engine warning lights.

    Our EGR Solutions include:

  • Professional cleaning or removal of the EGR system.
  • Recalibration of the ECU to ensure optimal performance.

    • Benefits:

  • Improved engine reliability and performance.
  • Enhanced fuel efficiency and smoother driving.
  • Eliminates common EGR-related faults and warning lights.

DPF Solutions

    DPF systems are essential for reducing emissions but can become blocked, leading to reduced engine performance and costly repairs.

    Our DPF Solutions include:

  • Regeneration of the DPF system.
  • Professional DPF removal with recalibrated ECU settings.

    • Benefits:

  • Restored engine power and fuel efficiency.
  • Prevents frequent DPF-related breakdowns.
  • Tailored to your needs while ensuring optimal engine performance.

Popcorn Limiter / Hardcut

    This performance tuning option modifies your vehicle's rev limiter to create a unique "popcorn" or crackling exhaust sound when revved to its limit.

    What we offer:

  • Customized tuning to enable the Popcorn Limiter or Hardcut effect.
  • Safe implementation without compromising engine health.

    • Benefits:

  • A distinct, sporty exhaust sound.
  • Tailored settings to suit your vehicle and preferences.
  • Enhanced enjoyment for enthusiasts who love a dynamic driving experience.

AdBlue Solutions

    AdBlue systems are essential for reducing NOx emissions in modern diesel vehicles, but issues with the system can lead to frequent maintenance, warning lights, and unnecessary costs.

    Our AdBlue Solutions include:

  • Diagnosis of AdBlue system faults.
  • Professional AdBlue system removal.

    • Benefits:

  • Eliminates costly AdBlue system errors.
  • Maintains consistent engine performance.
  • Custom solutions tailored to your vehicle and driving needs.
