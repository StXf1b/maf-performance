INFO SECTION

All ECU modifications start at a base price of €149.99. Additional modifications can be added for an extra cost, depending on the type and number of modifications required.

For example, if you purchase a Stage 1 remap, you are entitled to three free add-ons, such as EGR delete, DPF delete, or Popcorn/Hardcut, all for a total of €179.99 with no additional fees. However, if you choose only a single modification, like DPF delete or EGR delete, it will be priced at the base rate of €149.99.
