Car remapping is the process of adjusting the electronic control unit (ECU) in your car to modify engine parameters like fuel and air intake. This can increase horsepower, torque, and fuel efficiency, providing a smoother, more powerful drive.

Yes, when done correctly, car remapping is safe. At MAF Performance, our experts use advanced technology and follow manufacturer specifications to ensure each remap optimizes your vehicle’s performance without compromising safety or reliability.

DPF delete removes the diesel particulate filter, which can improve engine efficiency by reducing backpressure. EGR delete, on the other hand, disables the exhaust gas recirculation system to increase performance by allowing cooler, more oxygen-rich air into the engine.

A Stage 1 remap is designed for vehicles with no additional hardware modifications, providing a safe and efficient performance boost. This is a great option for those looking to enhance their car’s power and fuel efficiency without further upgrades.

Yes, at MAF Performance, we keep your car's original ECU settings backed up. If needed, we can restore your vehicle to its factory configuration at any time. Some extra costs might be included.
